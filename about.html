About KDN Security Services

Your trusted security partner in Botswana since 2013

Our Story

Dikgaka Trade (Pty)Ltd T/A KDN Security Services was founded in 2013 as a Gaborone-based manned guarding Security Company. From humble beginnings with just 2 people, the company has grown exponentially to more than 250 employees today.

Over the years, we have developed and implemented a comprehensive Quality Management System (QMS) to document company policies and procedures, meet client requirements and expectations, and continuously improve our service quality.

With our headquarters in Kopong and branches in Palapye, Mahalapye, and Maun, we are strategically positioned to serve clients across Botswana with prompt and professional security solutions.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading security service business establishments in Botswana, through a diverse professional skills set, and clarity of purpose and values.

Our Mission

To serve all clients by exceeding minimum acceptable quality standards and by providing the highest quality security services.
