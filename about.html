About KDN Security Services

KDN Security Services, founded in 2013, is one of Botswana’s most trusted security firms. From humble beginnings with just two employees, we have grown to over 250 strong, providing professional guarding, surveillance, alarm systems, and VIP protection across the country.

We operate 24/7 with fully equipped branches in Gaborone, Palapye, Mahalapye, and Maun. Our success is built on quality service, integrity, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation in security technology.

Our trained personnel, supported by a robust Quality Management System (QMS), deliver tailored security solutions to corporate, governmental, and residential clients.
